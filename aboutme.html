About Me

I am Clemente Gotelli, a Chilean hydraulic engineer with a PhD in Mechanics from EPFL in Switzerland. I have a comprehensive background in hydraulic engineering and water resources, with experience in both experimental work and numerical modeling using 2D and 3D software to study river processes and sediment dynamics.

I am currently based in Switzerland, where I continue developing projects and explorations in environmental hydraulics. This space brings together my personal and technical reflections, guided by curiosity and a genuine interest in understanding and communicating how natural systems work.
