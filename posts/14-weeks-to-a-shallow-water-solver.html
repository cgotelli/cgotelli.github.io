14 weeks to a Shallow Water solver

Hydraulics CFD

A structured 14-week roadmap to build a simple 2D shallow water solver from scratch. Each week introduces one focused step—numerics, stability, boundary conditions, friction, and visualization—so we reach a working prototype by the end.

Before we start

  • Install: Python 3.11+, NumPy, Matplotlib; optional: Jupyter, git, VS Code (or your preferred IDE).
  • Prereqs: basics of shallow water equations, calculus and linear algebra, finite differences, and comfort with Python scripting.
  • Goal: a clean, minimal solver you can extend (sediment transport, source terms, parallelization, GPU later if you like).
