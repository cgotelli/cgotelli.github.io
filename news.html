October 1

Started postdoc at STREEM Lab

I have begun a postdoctoral position at the Laboratory of Sustainable River Engineering, Energy, and Morphodynamics (STREEM) at EPFL under the supervision of Professor Mirko Musa.

The STREEM lab aims to advance the technological development of water-based energy production, understand its co-evolution with a changing climate, and protect riverine ecosystems by combining laboratory experiments, data analysis, and analytical methods.

I will be running OpenFOAM-based numerical simulations to study how bedforms in natural rivers interact with hydrokinetic turbines, supporting designs that boost performance while safeguarding the river environment.
